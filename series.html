Mandalorian

The story of Din Djarin, also known as Mandalorian, started with 1st season released in 2019 and has 3 seasons with the latest one relesead in 2023. The Mandalorian follows the journey of a lone bounty hunter in the outer reaches of the galaxy, set in the aftermath of the fall of the Empire and before the rise of the First Order. Tasked with capturing a mysterious and adorable alien creature known as "The Child" (or Baby Yoda by fans), Djarin develops a strong bond with the child and goes against his initial mission. The show explores themes of honor, loyalty, and the complexities of the Star Wars universe as Djarin navigates a dangerous and lawless galaxy while uncovering the child's significance and confronting various adversaries seeking to exploit its powers.

Ahsoka

Ahsoka Tano, the former Jedi apprentice of Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars, seeks to prevent the return of Grand Admiral Thrawn following the fall of the Empire. A respected leader and warrior attuned to the light side of the Force, Ahsoka grew into a formidable fighter before the Empire's reign changed the course of galactic history. Although she walked away from the Jedi Order, she continued to stand up for those fighting for peace and justice in the galaxy long after the fall of the Republic. The show had a huge success among Star Wars fan as there were a lot of beloved characters returning and new, exciting characters intoduced.
